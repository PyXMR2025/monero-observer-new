In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews Monero Talk[^4] show host Douglas Tuman[^5]:

> [..] would I rather have a million dollars in completely traceable currency or, you know, 500K dollars of completely untraceable currency? I was like [..] I want Monero. (Douglas @ 10:53)

Several topics are covered in this short interview, including: CBDC's, social credit scores, Monero DNM and mainstream adoption, CPU vs ASIC mining, and MoneroTopia 2023[^6].
