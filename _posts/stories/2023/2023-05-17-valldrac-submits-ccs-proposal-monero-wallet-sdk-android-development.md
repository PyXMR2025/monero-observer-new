valldrac[^1], the lead developer of Molly.im[^2], has submitted a new CCS proposal[^3] looking to develop a *Monero Wallet SDK for Android*[^4]:

> By funding this project we aim to accelerate the adoption of Monero in the Android ecosystem, providing a reliable and flexible solution for developers to integrate Monero into their apps. This funding will also drive the integration of Monero into Molly.im.

### Milestones

- M1: Development ($22,000)
- M2: Security Enhancements ($3,000)
- M3: Continuous Integration & Testing ($6,200)
- M4: Maintenance ($2,400 for 3 months)
- M5: Maintenance ($2,400 for 3 months)

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !388[^3].

Consult previous Monero Observer reports[^5]'[^6]'[^7] to learn more about the project.
