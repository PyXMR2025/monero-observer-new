In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews Oxen[^4] and Session[^5] *CTO*, Kee Jeffery[^6]:

> [..] I would assume like the speculative value over what people are actually using Monero for, is like, you know, probably 10 times what Monero is actually worth .. (Kee @ 38:35)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including the end-to-end encrypted Session messenger, decentralized exchanges, Monero mining, POS vs POW, and the Oxen network anonymity layer and private cryptocurrency.
