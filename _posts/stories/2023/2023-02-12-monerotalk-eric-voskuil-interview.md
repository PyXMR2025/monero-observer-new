In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews *Libbitcoin*[^4] lead developer Eric Voskuil[^5]:

> [..] banking is not gonna go away and lending shouldn't go away, you know that's fucking stupid [..] the only kind of lending is fractional reserve lending .. (Eric @ 1:54:03)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including Monero vs Bitcoin, fungibility and privacy, ASICs vs CPUs, centralization, and the future of crypto.

To learn more about the guest's work, visit his website[^6].
