Version 1.0.48[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and improvements:

- Design: light theme shadows.
- Feat: choose default tab sections that open when we launch the app.
- Feat: improve accessibility.
- Feat: adding a review widget to the chat and activity tab when the trade is completed.
- Fix: ads are loading on the ads screen.
- Fix: grey screen on the trade tab.
- Fix: notification icons on Android.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
