Version 1.0.46[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and improvements:

- Links for help in the chat.
- Fixed the Cancel button in the trade.
- Added new languages.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
