Cake Labs[^1] has announced[^2] the launch of their new *The Monero Minute* short news program:

> [The Monero Minute] covers the latest developments in the Monero and Crypto world. [..] Let's open source the direction of the show; rip it apart, put it back together, let's build together. Would love to know your thoughts friends.

The show debuted today on the Monero.com YouTube[^3], Instagram[^4] & TikTok[^5] channels.
