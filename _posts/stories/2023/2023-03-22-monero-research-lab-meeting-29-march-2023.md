The next Monero Research Lab[^1] meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, March 29th 2023 at 17:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place on IRC-Libera/Matrix[^2] in the #monero-research-lab channels.

### Agenda overview

- Discussion with paper author(s): "Range Proofs with Constant Size and Trustless Setup"[^3]
- Discuss: Consider removing the tx_extra field[^4]
- Discuss: Exploring Trustless zk-SNARKs for Monero's payment protocol[^5]
- Improvements to the decoy selection algorithm (MRL#86[^6], MRL#88[^7], Rucknium's OSPEAD[^8]) 
- Seraphis[^9]
- MRL META: "Cat herding"[^10] [..]

This meeting's chairperson will be UkoeHB[^11]. To view the full discussion topic list, consult #818[^12].

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^13].
