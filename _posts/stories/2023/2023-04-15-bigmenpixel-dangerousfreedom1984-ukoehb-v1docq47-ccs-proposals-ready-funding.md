Four CCS proposals[^1]'[^2]'[^3]'[^4] have been moved to the funding stage and are now looking for community support:

- BigmenPixel is looking to maintain the Monero GUI flatpak package for 1 year (10 XMR)[^5]
- DangerousFreedom1984 wants to continue development work on the Seraphis wallet for 8 weeks (31 XMR)[^6]
- UkoeHB is planning to focus on updating the Seraphis paper draft (163.7 XMR)[^7]
- v1docq47 wants to continue doing Russian voice-over work for the next 6 months (64.8 XMR)[^8]

You can support the proposals by donating to the XMR addresses listed on the *Funding Required* page.

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*

**Update 23/4/19: UkoeHB's and BigmenPixel's proposals fully funded.**

**Update 23/4/21: DangerousFreedom1984's proposal fully funded.**

**Update 23/4/30: v1docq47's proposal fully funded.**
