Lovera[^1]'s third CCS proposal[^2] to continue producing *quality educational content for the Spanish-speaking community* (Monero videos and articles) for 3 months is now ready for funding:

```
Funding needed: 10.5 XMR
```

To support the proposal, you can transfer XMR to the address listed on its Gitlab *Funding Required*[^3] page.

To learn more about this CCS, consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4].
