In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews ex-Darknet vendor Sam *DoingFedTime* Bent[^4]:

> [..] I got caught because I had specific policies and protocols that I implemented with my one employee which was my cousin, and she didn't follow them [..] that's what led to our big issue of being raided .. (Sam @ 39:50)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including Sam's story and philosophies, DNM tools, *Dark Gold*, PGP vulnerabilities, OPSEC and privacy.

To learn more about the guest's work, visit his video channel[^5] and *doingfedtime.com*[^6].
