In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews Magic Grants[^4] *President* and Cake Labs[^5] *VP of Operations*, Justin Ehrenhofer[^6]:

> [..] at the end of the day anything you try to build on Monero or try to make compatible with Monero needs to play nice with the overall mission of being fungible, digital cash .. (Justin @ 56:45)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including Monero ordinals and the pros and cons of removing *tx_extra*, steganography, poisoned outputs, fungibility, Seraphis and full membership proofs, and future Monero hard forks.
