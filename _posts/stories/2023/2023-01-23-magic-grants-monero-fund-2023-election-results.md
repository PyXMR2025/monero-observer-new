The *MAGIC Grants Board*[^1] has published the results[^2] for their *Monero Fund*[^3] 2023 election[^4]:

> MAGIC Grants used a ranked-choice voting method to select three new MAGIC Monero Fund committee members for 2 year terms. The election had an 52% voter turnout. [..] These members will join Rucknium and kayabaNerve on the committee on January 31.

### Committee members

- Rucknium[^5]
- kayabaNerve[^6]
- monerobull[^7]
- MajesticBank[^8]
- artlimber[^9]

Consult the previous Monero Observer reports[^10]'[^11]'[^12] to learn more about MAGIC and the election process.
