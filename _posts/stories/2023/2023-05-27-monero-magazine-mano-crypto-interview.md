In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], host Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews Coinpower[^4] CEO and self-proclaimed Monero extremist, Mano Crypto[^5], on private & sovereign money:

> [..] if the same thing happens to Monero what happened to Bitcoin, I'm out [..] I just want the best digital cash there is, and on this moment that's Monero .. (Mano @ 27:10)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: privacy, globalism and technocracy, CBDCs, social credit systems, digital IDs, and the biggest threats to XMR.
