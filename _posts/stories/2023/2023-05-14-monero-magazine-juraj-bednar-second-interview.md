In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], host Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews return guest Juraj Bednar[^4]:

> [..] we have found in Hacktrophy, the bug bounty program, that actually also most of the hackers want to get paid in crypto .. (Juraj @ 20:50)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: business crypto adoption, allocation strategies, crypto accounting, and fighting inflation. 

Monero Magazine previously interviewed Juraj in December 2022[^5].
