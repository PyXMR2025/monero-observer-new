Cake Labs[^1] has announced[^2] the release of the first Cake Wallet[^3] beta version for desktop:

> We are extremely excited to announce that Cake Wallet is now available on Apple Silicon (M1/M2) Macs as a beta version, with Linux and Windows to come shortly!
    
To help with testing, join the *TestFlight*[^4] and the public beta testers room on Telegram[^5].
