plowsof[^1] has submitted a new proposal[^2] looking to continue working as *CCS Coordinator* for 3 more months, after a successful[^3] *first term*[^4]:

> Hello, plowsof here, I show up and try to be helpful. My previous proposal happened, i would like to make it happen again, and do more of the same things.

```
Total funding: 69 XMR.

ETA: 3 months.
```

### Milestones

- M1: 2 meetings + hours worked (23 XMR)
- M2: 2 meetings + hours worked (23 XMR)
- M3: 2 meetings + hours worked (23 XMR)

### Tasks overview

- Gather consensus for CCS proposals
- Organise bi-weekly meetings to discuss proposals / current events
- Provide decisions in 1 month or less (Yes/No to being funded or if something has to be changed with the proposal to satisfy our community)
- Checking up on the WIP list to find / fix problems early
- Take responsibility for the entire CCS process 
- Act as a liaison to Core 
- Consultancy before / during / after the idea stage [..]

plowsof's contributions also include a General Fund donation alert bot (*monero-fund-watch*[^5]), a popular self-hosted wishlist as a service (WaaS)[^6], *monero-gui-exe*[^7], monerodevs.org[^8], and a Python script for Monero meeting moderators[^9].

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !385[^2].
