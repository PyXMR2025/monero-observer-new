DangerousFreedom1984[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] looking to continue developing the Seraphis[^3] wallet:

> Following the discussions on February 7, at the #no-wallet-left-behind group, I believe I can contribute by implementing the following wallet features [..]

```
Total funding: 31 XMR (20h/week).

ETA: 8 weeks.
```

### Proposed work overview

- Transaction history component
- Knowledge proofs integrated into wallet
- Some specific functions [..]

### Milestones

- M1: delivery of proposed wallet functions (31 XMR)


DangerousFreedom1984 is the maintainer of moneroinflation.com[^4]. Consult the dev's previous CCS proposals[^5]'[^6] to learn more about their work.

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !377[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*

**Update 23/4/21: proposal fully funded[^9].**
