Version 1.0.50[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with the following fixes and improvements:

- New feature: address book for wallets.
- Ads group selection in one click.
- Fixed Chinese translation.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
