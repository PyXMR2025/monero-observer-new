The preliminary schedule[^1] for the upcoming (June 23-25) *Monero Konferenco 2023*[^2] Prague event was announced today: 37 speakers[^3] and around 51 talks and other group sessions[^4] are currently listed on the website.

A limited number of *exclusive VIP tickets*[^5] are up for sale today, on Monero's 9th anniversary.

To get involved and learn more about the 3-day conference, join the next *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^6] and consult the associated CCS proposal[^7].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
