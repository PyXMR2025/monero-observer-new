Monero contributor and XMR.ru[^1] mod v1docq47[^2] has posted a new progress report (February 2022 - March 2023)[^3] for his CCS[^4] to do Russian voice-over work and transcribe Monero content:

> [..] this is my February + March CCS progress report with voiceover and works on XMR.RU. [..] If you have suggestions or advice about my work, I will be glad to listen to them. [..] Thanks for your support!

### Work summary

```
(A) Monero Russian Community YouTube Channel:
- Monero Weekly News (Issues #190-196)
- Monero Quarterly News (Issue #10)
- MoneroKon 2022 (RU voice-over, 12 x talks)
(B) Monero on XMR.RU:
- 7 x posts/articles
- Cypherpunk Transmission (Issues 001-006)
```

v1docq47 has also continued to update the Russian translation of new *Trocador.app* strings[^5] and completed translation of the *Zero-Knowledge Basics Cheatsheet*[^6] infographic.

The complete list of v1docq47's contributions can be found on Trello[^7]. 

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^8] to learn more about the associated CCS proposal.
