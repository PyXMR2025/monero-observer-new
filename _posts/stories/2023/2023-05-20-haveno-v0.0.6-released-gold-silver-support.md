Haveno[^1] version 0.0.6[^2] was released with support for gold and silver currencies, dark theme changes, and other updates and fixes.

### Changes overview

```
* add gold and silver
* refactor offer tabs to fiat, bitcoin, other (with gold and silver) 
* Changes on dark theme
* Moving away from JUnit 4 completely to JUnit 5 Jupiter
* Aggregate and send coverage report to Codacy
* Revert "Aggregate and send coverage report to Codacy"
* fix npe in offer payment account selection
```

The full changelog is available on Github[^3].
