The next *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled[^2] to take place on Saturday, January 21st 2023 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^3] Matrix & IRC channels and the agenda is available on Github.

### Agenda overview

- Greetings
- Update on Monerokon store and ticket waiting list
- Approve Sponsors
- Community approval of invoices[^4]
- ETC community participation
- Merchandise production plan [..]

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^5].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
