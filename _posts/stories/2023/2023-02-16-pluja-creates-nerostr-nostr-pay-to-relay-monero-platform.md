pluja[^1] has created Nerostr[^2], a *Nostr[^3] pay-to-relay Monero-based platform that anyone can selfhost*:

> Right now, 100% of the Nostr paid relays are being paid in Bitcoin (via LN). For this, I decided I would create a Monero-paid relay so the Monero community starts having a play in Nostr![^4]

The project's source code and self-hosting instructions are available on Codeberg[^5].

To support Nerostr, you can donate XMR to the address listed at the bottom of the *README.md*[^6] file.
