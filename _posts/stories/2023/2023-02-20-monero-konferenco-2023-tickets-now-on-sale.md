Tickets to the *MoneroKon 2023*[^1] annual conference are now on sale[^2] and available to the general public:

```
277 General Admission (€132.00)
20 VIP (€264.00)
```

Special tickets for kids, students, speakers, volunteers, and press passes are also available in the shop.

The 2-day event is set to take place in Prague, Czech Republic (Paralelni Polis/La Fabrika) on 23-25 June 2023.

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^3] to learn more about the MoneroKon23 CCS proposal[^4].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*

**Update: VIP tickets sold out[^5].**
