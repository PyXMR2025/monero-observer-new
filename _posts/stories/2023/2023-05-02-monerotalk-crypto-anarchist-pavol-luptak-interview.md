In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews crypto-anarchist and  Paralelní Polis[^4] co-founder, Pavol Lupták[^5]:

> [..] even if Monero doesn't become like the mainstream [..] it will be like the standard of the cypherpunk culture or alternative culture, so I'm more than sure that Monero will survive - I have no doubt. (Pavol @ 49:40)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including the life of a digital nomad and living off of Monero, acquiring residency in crypto friendly countries, Bitcoin privacy, the war on crypto and CBDCs.

To learn more about the guest, visit *opportunist.global*[^6].
