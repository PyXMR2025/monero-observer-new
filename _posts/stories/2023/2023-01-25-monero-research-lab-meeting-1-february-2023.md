The next Monero Research Lab[^1] meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, February 1st 2022 at 17:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place on IRC-Libera/Matrix[^2] in the #monero-research-lab channels.

### Agenda overview

- Discuss Jamtis address checksums.[^3]
- Improvements to the decoy selection algorithm (MRL#86[^4], MRL#88[^5], Rucknium's OSPEAD[^6]) 
- Seraphis[^7]
- MRL META: "Cat herding"[^8] [..]

This meeting's chairperson will be UkoeHB[^9]. To view the full discussion topic list, consult #790[^10].

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^11].
