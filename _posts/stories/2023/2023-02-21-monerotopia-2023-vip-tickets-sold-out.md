All *VIP Access* tickets ($299)[^1] to the *MoneroTopia23*[^2] conference have sold out[^3]. *General Admission* tickets ($25[^4]/$99[^5]) can still be purchased from *monerotopia.com*.

The 3-day event is set to take place in Mexico City (Huerto Roma Verde, Colonia Roma venue[^6]) on 5-7 May 2023.

Consult the website for more information about the schedule, confirmed speaker list, and accommodation suggestions.

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
