KuCoin[^1] has announced[^2] the temporary suspension of deposit and withdrawal services for Monero due to *node maintenance*:

> [..] for the safety of your account and assets, we have decided to suspend the deposit and withdrawal services for Monero (XMR).

The news coincides with KuCoin's KYC verification system *upgrade* notice[^3].

In terms of spot volume, KuCoin is the #2 XMR CEX after Binance.
