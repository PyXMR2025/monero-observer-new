The next Monero Research Lab[^1] meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, April 5th 2023 at 17:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place on IRC-Libera/Matrix[^2] in the #monero-research-lab channels.

### Agenda overview

- Discuss: Consider removing the tx_extra field[^3]
- Discuss: Exploring Trustless zk-SNARKs for Monero's payment protocol[^4]
- Improvements to the decoy selection algorithm (MRL#86[^5], MRL#88[^6], Rucknium's OSPEAD[^7]) 
- Seraphis[^8]
- MRL META: "Cat herding"[^9] [..]

This meeting's chairperson will be UkoeHB[^10]. To view the full discussion topic list, consult #822[^11].

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^12].
