Lovera[^1] has submitted a monthly progress report (March 2023)[^2] for his recent CCS proposal[^3] to produce *quality educational content for the Spanish-speaking community*.

### Work overview

- Monero News (Week 1-4, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
- Videos about Mining (YouTube)
- Video Tutorial/guide/review about Wallets, Nodes, Tools and others
- Video Questions FAQ about Monero in General
- Written content on the web (Multisig Wallets en Monero)[^4]

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^5] to learn more about Lovera's work.
