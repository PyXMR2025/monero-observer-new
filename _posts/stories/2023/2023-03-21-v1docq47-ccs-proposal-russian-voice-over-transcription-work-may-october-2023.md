Monero contributor and XMR.ru[^1] mod v1docq47[^2] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^3] to continue doing Russian voice-over work and transcribing Monero content for the next 6 months (May-October 2023):

> This is our new CCS proposal for a new work period for making Russian voiceovers of Monero Konferenco and Monerotopia video presentations and for the support of XMR.RU web-site

```
Funding needed: 64.8 XMR (6 months)
ETA: October 31 2023 (starting in May 2023) 
```

### Planned work overview

- Monero Konferenco 2022: finish the Russian voiceover of the remaining videos
- MoneroTopia 2023: start voiceover work for presentations
- All talks: publish text transcripts in Russian and English in a separate repository
- cover all key events happening in the Monero Community on a regular basis in a separate *news blog*[^4]
- XMR.ru: update/rewrite tutorials (wiki[^5])

A more complete list of planned work for this period can be found on Trello[^6]. 

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !382[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*

**Update 23/4/27: fully funded[^8].**
