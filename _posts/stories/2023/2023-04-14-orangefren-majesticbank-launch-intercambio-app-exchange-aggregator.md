The OrangeFren[^1] project has teamed up with MajesticBank[^2] to launch *Intercambio.app*[^3], a new *privacy-first* exchange aggregator that supports XMR: 

> Today we're announcing a brand new, privacy-first, no JS required, no KYC, exchange aggregator - Intercambio.app [..] Intercambio.app is jointly developed by OrangeFren.com & MajesticBank.sc, if you have feedback we'd love to hear it![^4]

Exchange fees are *around 0.5 to 2%*, and listed cryptocurrencies include BTC, BCH, WOW, ZEC, LTC, ETH, and USDT. In its current form, Intercambio.app is apparently *very similar*[^5] to the popular Trocador.app[^6]. 

It will be interesting to observe if the Monero community finds a way to see past MajesticBank's recent troubles[^7] and support this project.

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
