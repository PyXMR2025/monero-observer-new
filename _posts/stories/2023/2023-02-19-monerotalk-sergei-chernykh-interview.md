In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews *P2Pool*[^4] creator Sergei Chernykh[^5]:

> [..] [percentage of the network that's mined by bots] **yes, I would say yeah, at least 20%.** (Sergei @ 30:25)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including P2Pool news, decentralized mining, the future of XMR mining, botnets, and RandomX vs. ASICs.

Monero Talk has previously interviewed Sergei back in 2021[^6].
