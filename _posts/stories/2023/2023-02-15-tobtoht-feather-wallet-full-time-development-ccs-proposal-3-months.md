tobtoht[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] looking to continue working full time on *Feather Wallet*[^3] development for the next 3 months:

> Work for 40 hours per week for the next 3 months at a rate of €45/h. At €152 / XMR (14 day EMA) this makes 142 XMR.

```
Total funding: 142 XMR.

ETA: 3 months (TBD).
```

### Planned work overview

- work on issues/ideas reported by users
- work on tasks listed on the ideas page[^4]
- upstream useful patches to monero core
- test and review pull requests (GUI/core) [..]

tobtoht is the maintainer of Feather Wallet, a Monero core codebase contributor, and creator of *xmrguide*[^5]. He has been an active contributor to the Monero ecosystem since April 2018.

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !376[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
