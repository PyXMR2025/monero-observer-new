MajesticBank[^1] has donated 74.49995556 XMR (~$12K USD)[^2]'[^3] to the Monero General Fund address[^4], as promised yesterday[^5]:

> All fees earned from exchanges on 17th and 18th (two days) of April will be donated to Monero General Fund.

plowsof confirmed[^6] that the transfer is *legit* (not change) and the proof of payment[^7] submitted by MajesticBank is *valid*.

This is the second[^8] consecutive yearly GF donation from MajesticBank on Monero's birthday, and the exchange plans to continue the tradition for *many years*.

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
