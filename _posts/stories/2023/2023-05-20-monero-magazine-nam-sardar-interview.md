In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], host Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews Neel Capital[^4] founder, Nam Sardar[^5], on Monero's purpose:

> [..] it's not gonna be easy for people to find a way out, but [..] new roads and new solutions in life come about when there is necessity [..] Monero will get adopted when people really need it and when they realize there's not other way (Nam @ 20:20)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: Monero, proof of work, fungibility, Dubai's war on privacy, and the dangers of traceability.
