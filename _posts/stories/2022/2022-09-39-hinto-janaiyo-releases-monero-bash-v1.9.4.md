hinto-janaiyo[^1] has released version 1.9.4[^2] of the Monero Bash wrapper for Linux, *monero-bash*[^3] with multiple updates and bugfixes:

- Upgrade: verbose text is prettier
- Status: XMRig stats are more colorful
- Config: Update p2pool.conf max peers, 1000 -> 450
- Upgrade: Fixed verification data not being decompressed properly
- [..]

The full changelog is available on Github[^4].

The sources, SHA256SUM and .asc files can be found on Github[^2]. Always verify before using.

To learn more and support the project, consult hinto-janaiyo's new CCS proposal[^5] to finish developing monero-bash v2.
