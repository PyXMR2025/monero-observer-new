The next Monero Research Lab[^1] meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, September 21st 2022 at 17:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place on IRC-Libera/Matrix[^2] in the #monero-research-lab channels.

### Agenda

- Improvements to the decoy selection algorithm (MRL#86[^3], MRL#88[^4], Rucknium's OSPEAD[^5]) 
- Seraphis[^6]
- MRL META: "Cat herding"[^7] [..]

This meeting's chairperson will be UkoeHB[^8]. To view the full discussion topic list, consult #736[^9].

Summary and logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^10].
