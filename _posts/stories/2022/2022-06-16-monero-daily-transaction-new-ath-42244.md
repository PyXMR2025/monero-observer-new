Daily Monero transactions have reached a new all-time high yesterday with 42,244 confirmed, according to Moneroj.net's translog graph[^1]. 

The previous ATH of 40,768 was recorded on the 10th of November 2021.

This is part of a long-term upward trend that can be traced back to 2016.
