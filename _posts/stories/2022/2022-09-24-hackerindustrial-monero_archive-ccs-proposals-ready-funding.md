HackerIndustrial[^1]'s and monero_archive[^2]'s CCS proposals[^3]'[^4] have been moved to the funding stage after today's Monero Community Workgroup Meeting[^5]:

- HackerIndustrial is looking to build *MoneroSigner* (55.38 XMR)[^6]
- monero_archive wants to maintain a Monero Github code *archive* for 1 year (1.5 XMR)[^7]

You can support them by donating to the XMR addresses listed on the *Funding Required* page[^3]'[^4].

---

**Update 22/9/25: monero_archive's CCS proposal fully funded.**

**Update 22/10/5: HackerIndustrial's CCS proposal fully funded.**
