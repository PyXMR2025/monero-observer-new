The newest MyMonero[^1] mobile release, v1.3.4[^2], is out:

> Added additional information to in-app support messages.

This release is for both Android[^3] and iOS[^4]. 

Separate desktop[^5] and browser[^6] versions of the wallet are available on the official MyMonero website[^7].

To support the project, users can donate XMR to the address listed on Github[^8].

---

**Update: v1.3.5 was released with minor improvements and bug fixes[^9].**
