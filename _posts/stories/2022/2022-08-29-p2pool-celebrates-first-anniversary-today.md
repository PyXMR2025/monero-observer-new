P2Pool[^1], the decentralized Monero mining pool, is celebrating its first anniversary today:

> The first Monero block found by P2Pool was 2437679 on August 29th, 2021. So today we can celebrate 1 year since P2Pool started real mining on Monero blockchain. (sech1)[^2]

### Milestones

- Blocks found 12118
- 8365.547169569268 XMR paid out to miners in 943595 individual payouts
- The latest P2Pool release has been downloaded 15737 times so far
- 1489 active miners
- P2Pool is now 4th largest Monero pool with ~7% of total network hashrate

To learn more about P2Pool, read the official FAQ[^3] page and reach out to other miners on IRC[^4].
