Cake Labs[^1] has released *Cake Wallet* v4.4.7 and *Monero.com* v1.1.1[^2] with a hotfix for SimpleSwap[^3], their new exchange provider:

> We had an issue with SimpleSwap exchanges today with 4.4.6 and 1.1.0. These issues are now resolved for this version. 4.4.7 and 1.1.1 further improve reliability for SimpleSwap to ensure a smooth trading experience.[^4]

Consult the Github repository[^2] to verify and download the wallet APKs.

Note that Monero.com[^5] (iOS[^6], Android[^7]) is a Monero-only wallet and Cake Wallet[^8] (iOS[^9], Android[^10]) is a multi-coin wallet.
