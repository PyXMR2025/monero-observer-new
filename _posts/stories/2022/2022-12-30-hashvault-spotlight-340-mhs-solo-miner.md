HashVault.pro[^1] is, once more[^2], the top XMR mining pool, with ~39% of the total Monero network hashrate, after today's unexpected hashrate surge, from around 500 MH/s to over 1.1 GH/s.

Some Monero community members believe the ~340 MH/s *solo miner* can be indicative of *botnet activity*[^3].

P2Pool's HR is down ~4.3% for the day.
