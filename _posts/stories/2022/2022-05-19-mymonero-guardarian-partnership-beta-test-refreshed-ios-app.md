MyMonero[^1] has announced a new partnership with Guardarian[^2], as the team is getting ready to release the latest *refreshed iOS app*:

> We are very excited to announce our newest partnership with @guardarian_com [..] We can offer you the safest way to buy #Monero right within your #MyMonero wallet.[^3]'[^4]

> We are excited for this release as it brings our iOS app up to feature parity with our other apps.[^5]

Users can help beta test the upcoming MyMonero iOS app features by following instructions on the *Apple TestFlight*[^6] page. 

## Features to be tested

- Automatic migration of wallets from the old app to the new app
- Support for Monero-lws
- Send and receive transactions
- Payment Requests
- Creating Contacts
- Transfers to contacts

More details can be found in Tekkzbadger[^7]'s PSA[^5].
