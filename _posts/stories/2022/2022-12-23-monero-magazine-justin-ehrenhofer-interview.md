In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews Cake[^4]'s *VP of Operations* and MAGIC[^5] *President* Justin Ehrenhofer[^6]:

> [..] Monero needs people who actively care about privacy, Monero needs a strong community that is constantly pushing for improvements of the protocol, and pushing against potential changes that would result in a worse Monero .. (Justin @ 26:50)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: XMR DNM adoption, CBDC's, BTC Lightning privacy, social credit scores, and the fight against Monero.
