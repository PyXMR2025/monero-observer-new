Daily Monero transactions have reached a new ATH yesterday with 61,305 confirmed, according to Moneroj.net's translog graph[^1]. 

This is also an all-time high transaction percentage (~22.8%) against Bitcoin's total on-chain transactions (273,507)[^2] for the day. 

The previous XMR daily transactions record of 46,993[^3] was recorded last month, on the 19th of July.
