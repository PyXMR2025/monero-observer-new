In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews *Coin Center*[^4]'s *Research Director*, Peter Van Valkenburgh[^5], on the implications of the Tornado Cash case[^6] for the Monero ecosystem:

> [..] whether its fair or not, Monero has gotten a reputation for being more antagonistic to surveillance regulations [vs Zcash]  (Peter @ 1:00:35)

Several topics are discussed in this interview, including the history of legal issues around crypto, OFAC and the current state of AML regulations, Monero vs. Zcash and Bitcoin from a regulatory point of view and the applicability of free speech laws for computer code.
