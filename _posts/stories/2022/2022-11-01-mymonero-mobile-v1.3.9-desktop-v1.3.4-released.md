The newest *MyMonero for Mobile*[^1] release, v1.3.9[^2], and *MyMonero Desktop*[^3] v1.3.4[^4] are out:

> Bug fixes and minor improvements.

This mobile release is for both Android[^5] and iOS[^6]. 

The desktop[^3] and browser[^7] versions of the wallet are available on the official MyMonero website[^8].

To support the project, users can donate XMR to the address listed on Github[^9].
