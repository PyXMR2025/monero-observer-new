The Monero CLI[^1] & GUI[^2] wallets were included in the *first* I2P[^3]-based *Live OS*[^4] (Debian 11.5), according to *Opicaak*[^5]'s release announcement[^6]:

> I'm incredibly excited to introduce and share with all of you the first I2P based Live OS, the missing piece of I2P. [..] Money/Wallets: Official Monero Wallet (both CLI and GUI)

The ISOs[^4] (*LiveOS-x86_64-1.0.0.iso* and *LiveOS-x86_64-1.0.0-Exploratory-Edition.iso*) can be downloaded with any torrent client.

File hashes, hardware requirements, known issues, the complete list of included software, and other details are available in the announcement post[^6].

Note that no project websites are currently available. Feedback can be sent to Opicaak via email[^7], Irc2P[^8] and Mastodon[^5].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
