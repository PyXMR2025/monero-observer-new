John Foss[^1] has published Issue #57[^2] of *The Monero Moon* curated weekly newsletter:

> Please note, The Monero Moon will be taking a break throughout the month of September, and will hopefully return on October 6th 2022.

The weekly publication includes news about:

- Development, Releases, and Technology
- General News
- Events
- Exchanges and Merchants
- Community Crowdfunding
- Trading & Speculation
- Performance
- Entertainment

To support the author, you can donate XMR to the address listed in the footer of each issue.

If you enjoy reading *The Monero Moon*, consider supporting John's recent CCS proposal[^3] to fund the newsletter until January 2023.
