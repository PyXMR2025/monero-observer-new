DangerousFreedom1984[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] to help develop a *simple* Seraphis[^3] proof of concept CLI wallet and the audit framework:

> [..] I would like your help to develop more features for Seraphis like the audit framework and a simple CLI wallet. [..] I believe we could have a basic PoC CLI wallet by the end of January/23 and enough achievements to move to testnet.

```
Total funding: 120 XMR (25h/week).

ETA: 18 weeks.
```

### Milestones

- M1: Audit framework Seraphis (20 XMR)
- M2: PoC CLI Wallet (100 XMR)


DangerousFreedom1984 is the maintainer of moneroinflation.com[^4]. Consult the dev's previous CCS proposal[^5] to learn more about their work.

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !344[^2].
