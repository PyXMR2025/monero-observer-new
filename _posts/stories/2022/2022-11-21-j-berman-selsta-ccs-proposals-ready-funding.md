j-berman[^1]'s and selsta[^2]'s CCS proposals[^3]'[^4] have been moved to the funding stage and are now looking for community support:

- j-berman wants to continue full time development work on Monero for 3 months (480 hours, 253 XMR)[^5]
- selsta wants to continue working part-time on Monero development for 3 more months, until the end of January 2023 (360 hours, 135 XMR)[^6]

You can support them by donating to the XMR addresses listed on the *Funding Required* page[^3]'[^4].

---

**Update 22/11/22: selsta's CCS was fully funded (13 contributors/135.17 XMR).**

**Update 22/11/25: j-berman's CCS was fully funded (53 contributors/253.17 XMR).**
