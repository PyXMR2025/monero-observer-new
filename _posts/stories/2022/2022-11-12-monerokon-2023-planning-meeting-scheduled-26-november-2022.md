The next *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled to take place on Saturday, November 26th 2022 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^2] Matrix & IRC channels ~~and the agenda will soon be posted on Github.~~

### Agenda overview

- Review draft CFP[^3]
- Confirm Selection-Rejection Criteria[^4]
- MoneroKon 2023 Branding[^5]
- Confirm Invoice Payment[^6] and Speaker Expenses[^7] protocols
- Finalization of 2022 accounts[^8]
- CCS draft

Note that venue option *C) Prague* was chosen by the community in unanimous vote during the previous meeting:

> C) Prague, but fall back to Neuchâtel if funding target not met by a set deadline. If target does not cover Neuchâtel expenses, forward all funds to 2024

Consult the previous meeting logs[^9] for more information. 

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
