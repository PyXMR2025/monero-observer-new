Monthly Monero transactions have reached a new all-time high last month (July 2022) with 963,209 confirmed, according to Moneroj.net's *transmonth*[^1] graph:

> It had 963209 transactions in July, up from 885569 in June. (CryptoMorpheus_[^2])

XMR has also hit a new ATH monthly transaction percentage vs BTC for the second consecutive month:

> Against Bitcoin, it had in July 12.5% of transactions, up from 11.8% last month.

Access Moneroj.net's *percentmonth*[^3] graph for more information.
