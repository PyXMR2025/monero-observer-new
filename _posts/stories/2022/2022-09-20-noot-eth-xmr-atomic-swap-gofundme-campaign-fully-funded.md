noot[^1]'s ETH-XMR atomic swap development[^2] *GoFundMe* campaign[^3] organized by *MAGIC Monero Fund*[^4] was fully funded:

```
$24,058 USD raised of $24,000 goal (80 donations)
```

According to MRL's Rucknium[^5], this *development effort will make ETH<>XMR atomic swaps more usable for ordinary users*[^6].

Consult the previous Monero Observer reports[^7]'[^8] to learn more about the project and MAGIC.
