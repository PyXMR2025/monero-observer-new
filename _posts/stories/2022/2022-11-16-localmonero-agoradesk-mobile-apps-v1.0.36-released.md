Version 1.0.36[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and improvements:

- Feat: improved translations.
- Fix: assessibility buttons.
- Feat: added brute-force attack captcha
- Feat: open a trade screen after the trade initiated.
- Fix: changed dispute label color.
- Fix: FOSS version can't logout.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
