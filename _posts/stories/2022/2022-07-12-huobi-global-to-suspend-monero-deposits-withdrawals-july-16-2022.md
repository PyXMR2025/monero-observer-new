Huobi Global[^1] cryptocurrency CEX, seemingly unaware of the new v15 HF date[^2], has just  announced[^3] plans to suspend Monero deposits and withdrawals on July 16 at 23:00 (UTC), instead of August 13:

> Due to the XMR mainnet upgrade, Huobi Global will be suspending XMR deposits and withdrawals at 23:00 (UTC) on July 16, 2022.

The exchange has been made aware of the issue by Monero Observer.

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
