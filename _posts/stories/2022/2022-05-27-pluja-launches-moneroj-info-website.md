pluja[^1] has launched the new *moneroj.info*[^2] independent Monero knowledge site, a place for *useful articles about Monero for newbies and non-tech savvy people*:

> I don't aim to reinvent the wheel. I will be using and linking any content that has already been created, and it can be useful. But I will always try to create my very own original content and add value.[^3]

## Articles

- Introduction to Monero and transparent blockchains[^4]
- The risks of transparent blockchains[^5]

pluja plans to translate the website to Spanish, French and German.

To learn more about pluja's work, consult my previous reports[^6]'[^7]'[^8].
