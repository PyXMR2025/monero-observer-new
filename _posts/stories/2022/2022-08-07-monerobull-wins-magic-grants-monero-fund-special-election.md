monerobull[^1], the founder of monerosupplies.com[^2], has won *MAGIC Grants*[^3]'s *special election*[^4] for the *MAGIC Monero Fund*[^5], and will be filling the committee seat that was recently vacated by Vik Sharma[^6]:

> monerobull has been selected to join the MAGIC Monero Fund committee. They will serve the remainer of a 1-year term ending in January 2023.[^7]

Consult the previous Monero Observer reports[^4]'[^8]'[^9] to learn more about MAGIC and this election.
