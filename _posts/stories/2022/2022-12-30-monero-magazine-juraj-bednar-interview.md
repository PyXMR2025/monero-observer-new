In this Monero Magazine[^1] episode[^2], host Michael Fitzgerald[^3] interviews crypto and open source software enthusiast Juraj Bednar[^4]:

> I don't think it depends in any way on regulators [..] for me, it is okay if Monero stays with people who want to use it [..] I don't think we need mainstream adoption in order for Monero to succeed .. (Juraj @ 29:35)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: XMR DNM adoption, BTC privacy and LN scalability, Chainalysis and surveillance threats.
