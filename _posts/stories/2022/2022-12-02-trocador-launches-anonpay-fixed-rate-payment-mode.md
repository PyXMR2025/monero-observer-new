CryptoMorpheus_[^1] has announced[^2] the launch of *AnonPay*, a new *fixed rate Payment mode* on Trocador.app[^3]:

> Now you can create trades to receive specific amounts of any listed coin, and we'll generate fixed trades for you. [..] This payment method is also available on our API system, so any wallets and apps can also take advantage of that system.

The privacy-focused exchange aggregator has also listed three new coins: Cosmos (ATOM), Ergo and Flux.

To learn more about Trocador, consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
