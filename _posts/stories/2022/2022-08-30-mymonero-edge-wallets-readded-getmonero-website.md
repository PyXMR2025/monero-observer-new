The *MyMonero*[^1] and *Edge*[^2] lightweight XMR wallets were readded (#2035[^3]) to the official Getmonero.org website *Downloads*[^4] section.

Both wallets are now compatible with the recent Monero network upgrade[^5].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^6] to learn more about this story.
