nightwolf361[^1] has submitted a CCS proposal[^2] looking to create *Empower Lebanon*, an *educational and bartering platform*:

> Coding the website will take around a month to have a fully functional platform. [..] Mainly its an educational marketplace since the area is ravaged by the crisis, and spread replacing day to day fiat usage for XMR.

```
Total funding proposed: 50 XMR.

ETA: 2 months.
```

### Milestones

- M1: Posting stickers/printing flyers to distribute around Beirut to get more attention (guerilla marketing); arabic translation will be provided (5 XMR)
- M2: Design the UI/UX for the webapp (10 XMR)
- M3: Write the codebase for the website(back and front-end) (20 XMR)
- M4: Work on relevant content to educate on why cash is trash, the downfall of banks and why Monero is true digital cash (sound money, safe mode) (10 XMR)
- M5: Throw a Monero themed gathering for XMR fanatics (5 XMR)

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !367[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*

**Update: proposal closed *due to unclear intentions/negative interest*[^3].**
