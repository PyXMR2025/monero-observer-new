In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman interviews Duncan White[^3], a candidate for Braddon[^4] that is running for office on the democratic libertarian line (Liberal Democratic Party[^5]) to *save Australia from dystopia*:

> It would be great if it [privacy] could be default if it didn't come at a cost (42:40)

Several topics are covered in this interview, such as: political scene, crypto regulations and libertarians organizations in Australia, the importance of Monero and privacy and CBDCs.
