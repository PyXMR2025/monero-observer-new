Version 1.0.39[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and improvements:

- HotFix: captcha loop.
- Feat: assessibility improvements.
- Feat: maintenance mode when backend is not available.
- Fix: bug with the ad creation.
- Feat: when the ad is created, display message.
- Fix: cancel button for seller in disputed trades.
- Fix: able to open trade chat without PIN.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
