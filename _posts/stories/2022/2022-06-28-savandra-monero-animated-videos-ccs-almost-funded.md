savandra[^1]'s CCS proposal[^2] to create 5 Monero *animated explainer videos* is almost fully funded, mainly thanks to a few recent generous donations accounting for more than 3/4 of the total funding goal:

```
Raised 43.51 of 45 XMR (~96%) Contributions: 104
```

To contribute to the project, you can donate XMR to the address listed on the project's CCS page[^2].

To learn more about the proposal, consult the previous Monero Observer report[^3].
