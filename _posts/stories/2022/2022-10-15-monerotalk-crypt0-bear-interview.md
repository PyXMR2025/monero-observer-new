In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews the popular Monero artist and contributor, *Crypt0-bear*[^4]:

> [..] Monero is the spiritual successor of what Bitcoin was supposed to be. (Crypt0-bear @ 10:51)

Several other topics are also discussed in this interview, including Crypt0-bear's music and future projects, XMR vs BTC, privacy stablecoins, and the importance of cash.

Crypt0-bear's albums are available to download for free on the dark web[^5]. Consult the previous Monero Observer reports to learn more about his work[^6]'[^7].
