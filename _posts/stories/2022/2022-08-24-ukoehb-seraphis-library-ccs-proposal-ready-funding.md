UkoeHB[^1]'s CCS proposal[^2] to build the core Seraphis library is now ready for funding[^3]:

```
23 contributors raised ~82 of 122 XMR (~67%)
```

To support this proposal, you can donate XMR to the address listed on its Gitlab *Funding Required*[^3] page.

To learn more about the project, consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4].

---

**Update 22/8/26: Vik Sharma from Cake Labs donated 33 XMR to this CCS proposal[^5].**

**Update 22/8/27: binaryFate contributed 30% of this proposal (36.6 XMR) from the General Fund[^6].**

**Update 22/8/31: fully funded[^7].**
