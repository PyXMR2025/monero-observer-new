hinto-janaiyo[^1]'s CCS proposal[^2] to finish developing *Gupax*[^3], a cross-platform GUI for mining XMR on P2Pool, is now ready for funding[^4]:

```
Funding needed: 100 XMR
```

To support the proposal, you can donate XMR to the address listed on its Gitlab *Funding Required*[^4] page.

To learn more about this CCS, consult the previous Monero Observer report[^5].

---

**Update: fully funded[^6].**
