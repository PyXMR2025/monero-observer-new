Monero contributor and XMR.ru[^1] mod v1docq47[^2] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^3] to continue creating videos and translating Monero content into Russian for 6 months:

> Localization of the Monero project into Russian. Creation / voiceover of information / news and tutorials video for YouTube channel.

```
Funding needed: 85 XMR (6 months)
ETA: January 2023 (starting in August 2022) 
```

### Planned work

- Monerotopia 2022 voiceover and transcriptions (RUS + ENG)
- Monero Konferenco 2022 voiceover and transcriptions (RUS + ENG)
- Monero News (Weekly)
- Monero News (Quarterly)
- Monero Research Lab Papers
- Monero Meeting Log translations
- Support translations for Weblate (Russian)

A more complete list of the planned work for this period can be found on Trello[^4]. 

v1docq47 is also working on the *Unofficial Russian technical documentation for Monero*[^5]  project. Other contributions can be found on Weblate[^6] and the previous CCS proposal[^7]. 

You can post your comments and support this proposal on Gitlab in !329[^3].
