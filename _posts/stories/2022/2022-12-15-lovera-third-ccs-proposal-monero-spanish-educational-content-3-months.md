Lovera[^1] has submitted his third CCS proposal[^2] to continue producing *quality educational content for the Spanish-speaking community* (Monero videos and articles) for 3 months:

> Hi Monero community, Lovera here! this is my third proposal for my work. As you know I am dedicated to create content about Monero in Spanish, in this period I would like to dedicate more time to Monero so I would like to have the support of the community.

```
Total funding proposed: 10.5 XMR.

ETA: 3 months.
```

To learn more about Lovera's previous work, visit the *LoveraTV*[^3] video channel and the satishilov.com[^4] Monero blog.

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !366[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
