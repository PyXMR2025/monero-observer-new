The *Crypto Vigilante*[^1] video channel has uploaded a MoneroKon22[^2] backstage interview[^3] in which Cake[^4]'s Justin Ehrenhofer[^5] discusses Monero with host Rafael LaVerde[^6]:

> [..] you need to take some accountability [..] for telling people to use a tool [Bitcoin] that will result in people going to jail (Justin @ 26:50)

Several topics are covered in this interview, including: Monero regulation and policy, crypto compliance, KYC, centralized vs. decentralized exchanges, fungibility, P2P, and Bitcoin's privacy shortcomings.
