Haveno[^1] has released version 0.0.2[^2] of its test network:

> Follow the instructions, especially if you already tested v0.0.1, as there are some breaking changes.[^3]

### Changes overview

- Add double spend protection by decrypting payment info after first confirmation.
- Close arbitrator trade by notifying when trade completes.
- Upgrade to Gradle 7.3.3.
- Validate offer signer and signature in legacy UI.
- Show wallet transaction dates. [..]
