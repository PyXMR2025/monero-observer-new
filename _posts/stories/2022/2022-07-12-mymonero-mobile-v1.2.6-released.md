The newest MyMonero[^1] mobile release, v1.2.6[^2] is out and it comes with several improvements and bug fixes:

> Added several bug fixes and improvements. Improvements to handle CORS errors when using a custom LWS server.

This release is for both Android[^3] and iOS[^4]. 

Separate desktop[^5] and browser[^6] versions of the wallet are available on the official MyMonero website[^7].

To support the project, users can donate XMR to the address listed on Github[^8].

---

**Update 22/7/13: versions 1.2.7 and 1.2.8[^9] released with error fixes.**
