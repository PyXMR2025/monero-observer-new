BitMart[^1] cryptocurrency exchange has just listed[^2] a new XMR/USDT[^3] trading pair on their platform:

> BitMart will list Monero (XMR) on our digital assets platform on Dec. 30, 2022. The following trading pair will be available: XMR/USDT.

*Note: this is a centralized exchange and it can not respect your privacy. KYC measures will be enforced on its users[^4]. Use Atomic Swaps/DEX/P2P exchanges instead, if possible.*
