sausagenoods[^1] has submitted a CCS proposal[^2] looking to create *Metronero*[^3], a *Javascript free and Tor friendly* XMR checkout system that *uses merchant provided templates*:

> We would like to help with the adoption of Monero by providing a system similar to those of Revolut, PaySera, PayPal and Wise.

```
Total funding: 100 XMR (0.25 XMR/h).

ETA: ~10 weeks.
```

### Milestones overview

- M1: Planning the backend, APIs (admin, merchant account management),
multi-language support. [..] (5 weeks - 50 XMR)
- M2: Commission system for shared instance operators. [..] (5 weeks - 50 XMR)

A stagenet demo for the Metronero PoC is available at *metronero.digilol.net*[^4]. 

It is worth noting that sausagenoods and Laurynas[^5] are also the dev team behind the MoneroPay[^6] payment processor.

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !353[^2].
