Justin Berman[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] to continue development work on Monero for 3 more months:

> I'd like to continue full-time another 3 months in a similar capacity [..]

```
Funding proposed: 187.5 XMR (0.12 XMR/hr + $36/hr).

ETA: 3 months (480 hours).
```

### Tasks overview

- Continue with PR review (especially on larger time-intensive PR's) [..]
- Patch bugs.
- Finish the background sync mode that enables scanning for txs without a spend key. [..]
- (potential) Start going deeply through Seraphis code, implementing Jamtis features [..]
- (potential) Work together with @endogenic on factoring wallet2.
- [..]

The complete list of tasks is available on Gitlab[^2].

To learn more about Justin's work, consult his previous CCS proposal[^3], dev progress report[^4] and associated PRs[^5] on Github.

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !330[^2].
