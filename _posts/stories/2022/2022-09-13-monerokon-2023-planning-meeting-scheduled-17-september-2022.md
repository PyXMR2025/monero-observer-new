The first *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled to take place on Saturday, September 17 2022 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^2] Matrix & IRC channels.

## Agenda

```
Greetings
Monero 2022 status (midipoet+DR)
MoneroKon 2023 status (ajs)
MoneroKon 2023 - Role Pledging
MoneroKon 2023 - Venue selection
MoneroKon 2023 - Kanban
AOB
Confirm next meeting time and date
```

To view the full list of discussion topics and propose agenda items, consult MRL *meta* issue#733[^3] and the MoneroKon 2023 Kanban[^4].

*Note that the Monerokon Telegram group is no longer active, according to michaesc[^5].*
