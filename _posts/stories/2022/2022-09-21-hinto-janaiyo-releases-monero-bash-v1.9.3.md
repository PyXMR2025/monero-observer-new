hinto-janaiyo[^1] has released version 1.9.3[^2] of the Monero Bash wrapper for Linux, *monero-bash*[^3] with multiple updates and bugfixes:

- Status: Added x/month, x/year, user to P2Pool stats
- Status: Added disk left, synced, white/grey peers to Monero stats
- Wallet: All wallet types can now be created [..]
- Config: Added more options to monerod.conf & monero-wallet-cli.conf
- Status: Fixed P2Pool Latest share showing 1st share found
- Tor: Errors are more detailed and fixes are suggested
- [..]

The full changelog is available on Github[^4].

The sources, SHA256SUM and .asc files can be found on Github[^2]. Always verify before using.

To learn more and support the project, consult hinto-janaiyo's new CCS proposal[^5] to finish developing monero-bash v2.
