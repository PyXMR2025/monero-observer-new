ForgotSudo[^1] has submitted a CCS proposal[^2] to build *Merkato*[^3], a Monero marketplace plugin for the peer-to-peer *Onionr*[^4] *gossip network*:

> Hello, we are the Onionr team from VoidNetwork LLC and we would like to propose funding for the development of the Onionr marketplace. [..] The goal of this CCS is to continue the funding that is needed to build out a P2P Monero marketplace.

```
Total funding: 75 XMR.

ETA: TBD.
```

### Milestones

- M1 - Implement a web of trust system (23 XMR)
- M2 - Finish the marketplace plugin (45 XMR)
- M3 - Documentation (7 XMR)

To learn more about Onionr and Merkato, consult the associated WiP website[^4] and git repository[^5].

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !340[^2].

**Update: proposal closed[^6].**
