selsta[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] to continue working part-time on Monero development for 3 more months, until the end of January 2023:

> Work for 30 hours per week over the next 3 months (from November to end of January) at a rate of 45€ / hour. At 150€ / XMR (14 day EMA) this makes 108 XMR.

```
Total funding: 135 XMR.

ETA: (end of) January 2023.
```

He plans to focus on *setting up community node infrastructure*[^3], testing and reviewing PR's, Monero release engineering and other smaller dev work on the CLI and GUI.

selsta has contributed over 595 merged commits to both the CLI[^4] and GUI[^5] codebases since 2018.

You can post your comments and support this proposal on Gitlab in !354[^2].
