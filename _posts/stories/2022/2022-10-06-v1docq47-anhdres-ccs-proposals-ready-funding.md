v1docq47[^1]'s and anhdres[^2]'s CCS proposals[^3]'[^4] have been moved to the funding stage:

- v1docq47 is looking to do Russian voice-over work and transcribe Monero content for the next 6 months (42.8 XMR)[^5]
- anhdres wants to create the *Monero Garden* educational website (71 XMR)[^6]

You can support them by donating to the XMR addresses listed on the *Funding Required* page[^3]'[^4].

---

**Update 22/10/13: andhres's CCS proposal was fully funded[^7].**

**Update 22/10/18: v1docq47's CCS proposal was fully funded[^8].**
