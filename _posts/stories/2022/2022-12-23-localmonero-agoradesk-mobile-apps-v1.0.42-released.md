Version 1.0.42[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and improvements:

- Feat: a new way of delivering push notifications, solved iOS issue with disappearing notifications when the app is terminated.
- Fix: remove the Cancel button on the buyer side in disputed trades.
- Feat: disable trades polling when FCM is available, get info from push notifications.
- Feat: display sticky bubble for big screens when a keyboard is opened.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
