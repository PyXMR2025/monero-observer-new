UkoeHB[^1] has submitted a new *long-term* CCS proposal[^2] to continue working on additional *Seraphis library*[^3] tasks:

> Hi all, I closed out my previous Seraphis Library Work CCS after consuming all the hours. There are additional tasks I would like to work on. [..] In the previous CCS I finished adding new things to the seraphis library, and from here on mainly have cleanup tasks left.

```
Total funding: 130 XMR (50 USD + 0.2 XMR Rate).

ETA: TBD (240hrs total).
```

### Tasks overview

- Complete the 'final draft' of my seraphis library (finish my cleanup and review pass).
- Update the Seraphis paper draft.[^4]

To learn more about UkoeHB's work, consult the most recent dev update[^5], the *Preliminary Seraphis Design Overview*[^6] document, and the previous Monero Observer report[^7].

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !369[^2].
