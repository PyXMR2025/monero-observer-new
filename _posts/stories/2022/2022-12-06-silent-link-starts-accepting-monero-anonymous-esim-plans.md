Silent Link[^1] has started accepting[^2] Monero for their *instant anonymous eSim* plans:

> #XMR now accepted at silent.link

Plans start from $9 and the provider has a *Zero KYC policy*: no personal information is required to use the service, not even an email address. 

Furthermore, they promise to *never block Tor* or force users to *look for stoplights*.

Consult the FAQ[^3] page to learn more about compatible devices, other payment methods, and privacy and security benefits.
