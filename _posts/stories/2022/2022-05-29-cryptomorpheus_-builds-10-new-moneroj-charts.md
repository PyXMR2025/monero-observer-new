CryptoMorpheus_[^1] has built 10 new charts for Moneroj.net[^2]:

> In the mean time, since proposing this on May 11, I already built 10 new charts from that list.[^3]

### New charts

- Monero Marketcap dominance (against all coins)[^4]
- Coinmarketcap Ranking[^5]
- Merchants acceptance (total numbers, clearnet)[^6]
- Merchants increase (absolute numbers, clearnet)[^7]
- Merchants increase (%, clearnet)[^8]
- Coincards usage[^9]
- Daily Reddit Posts[^10]
- Daily Reddit Comments[^11]
- Darknet adoption (DREAD forum users)[^12]
- Privacy marketcap (all privacy coins)[^13]


To learn more about Moneroj.net and support the project, consult the associated CCS proposal[^14] and share your feedback in !318[^15].
