CryptoMorpheus_[^1] has published an XMR adoption metrics report[^2] on Moneroj.net[^3] for the month of September 2022:

> Hey guys, just updated the monthly adoption metrics on Moneroj.net . Check it out, new article.[^4]

The report references several charts, including merchants acceptance percentage[^5], Coincards usage[^6], Dread subscribers[^7], and monthly transactions[^8].

To support CryptoMorpheus_'s work and keep the Moneroj.net project free for everyone, donate XMR to the address listed on the About[^9] page.
