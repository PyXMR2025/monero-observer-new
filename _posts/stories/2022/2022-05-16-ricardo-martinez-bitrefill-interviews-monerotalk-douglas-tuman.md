Bitrefill[^1]'s Ricardo Martinez has interviewed[^2] Monero Talk[^3] host Douglas Tuman[^4] on Monero:

> Monero, I believe, is basically liberty through encryption, liberty through code. It's a way to, hopefully, guarantee our liberty in the digital age. (00:55)

Several topics are covered in this interview, such as: RandomX, XMR mining, darkweb adoption, surveillance, regulations and financial censorship, and *Monero as a backup plan to the coming cashless world of cbdcs*.
