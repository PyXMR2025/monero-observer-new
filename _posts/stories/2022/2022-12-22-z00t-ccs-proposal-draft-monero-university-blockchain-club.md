z00t[^1] has submitted a CCS proposal (draft)[^2] looking to introduce *Blockchain Club* college students to Monero:

> [..] I am reaching out to you all here in the hope that you all would see the importance of spreading information to college students, and would be willing to financially support such a venture for the next 12 months.


```
Total funding proposed: 20 XMR.

ETA: 12 months.
```

### Milestones

- M1: Introduce current members to XMR (5 XMR)
- M2: Begin spreading word throughout campus of XMR benefits (3 XMR)
- M3: Onboard at least 10 new members (5 XMR)
- M4: Expand our evangelism of XMR to 5 sister state universities (7 XMR)

To share your feedback, ask questions and support this proposal, consult !368[^2]. 

Note that the actual proposal body is currently only available in z00t's repository[^3]. 

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
