LocalMonero[^1] has announced[^2] the release of the *AgoraDesk* (APK[^3]/Android[^4]/iOS[^5]) and *LocalMonero* (APK[^6]/Android[^7]/iOS[^8]) mobile apps into *open beta testing* in celebration of their 5-year anniversary:

> It was 5 years ago that we launched our platform and announced it on this reddit thread that kicked off what would become one of the biggest P2P crypto trading platforms out there. [..] To celebrate, we are releasing our mobile apps into open beta testing today![^2]

The open-source[^9] apps were tested on GrapheneOS[^10] and should also work with other *privacy-centric and security-centric mobile OS distributions* like CalyxOS[^11] and LineageOS[^12]. 

Beta testers can join the LocalMonero groups on Matrix[^13] and Telegram[^14].
