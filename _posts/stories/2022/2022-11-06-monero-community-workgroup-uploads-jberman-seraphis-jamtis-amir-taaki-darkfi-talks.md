The *Monero Community Workgroup* video channel[^1] has uploaded two more talks recorded on June 19 at the *Monero Konferenco 2022* Lisbon event (Day 2):

- *Seraphis/Jamtis: Feature overview of the proposed upgrade to Monero*[^2] (by Justin Berman[^3]) 
- *DarkFi: Anonymous Engineering for Dank Net Markets*[^4] (by Amir Taaki[^5]).

The playlist[^6] currently includes 16 videos from the conference.

The complete list of talks and associated slides (.pdf) are available in MoneroKon's meta[^7] repository.
