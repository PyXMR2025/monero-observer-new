Cake Labs[^1] has announced[^2] their new *business-focused* website, *cakelabs.com*[^3]:

> Introducing our new business-focused website cakelabs.com! Please reach out to partners@cakelabs.com if you have a business inquiry for Cake Wallet, Monero.com, and Cake Pay
    
The Cake Wallet and Monero.com apps were moved to the Nevis company earlier this year, as previously reported[^4].
