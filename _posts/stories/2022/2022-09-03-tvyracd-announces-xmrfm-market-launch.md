tvyracd[^1] has announced[^2] the launch of the *XMR Free Market* (XMR F.M.[^3]) platform:

> Hello, we are happy to announce the launch of one of the first XMR legal marketplace for Monero enthusiasts and merchant brand vendors worldwide.

### Features overview

- Two-factor authentication
- Auto encrypt
- PIN
- Vendor profile with feedback and message system
- Monero Payment only

Note that an account is currently required to browse the website.
 
Read the announcement[^2] for more information about xmrfm.com.
