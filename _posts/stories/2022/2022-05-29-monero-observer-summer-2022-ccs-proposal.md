The Monero Observer CCS proposal[^1] for Summer 2022 is ready for community review:

> I will continue to maintain Monero Observer (https://monero.observer) for the next 3 months (Summer 2022): June, July and August.

After this period, Monero Observer will turn 1, marking 365 days of uninterrupted daily Monero news reporting. 

To support the proposal, submit your feedback in !322[^1].

### What's new

- weekly Monero Dev Activity Reports[^2]
- on demand Monero Workgroup Meeting Log Summary Reports[^3]
- Monero Observer self-hosted CLI Git server[^4]
- Monero Observer hidden service[^5]
- List of email service providers for anons[^6]
- XMR Stats section, homepage menu (more in the changelog[^7])

Thanks for being a loyal reader and supporter, I am very proud to be part of the best community out there! Stay cypherpunk.
