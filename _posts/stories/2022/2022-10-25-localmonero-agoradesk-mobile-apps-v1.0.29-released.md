Version 1.0.29[^1] of the *LocalMonero/AgoraDesk*[^2]'[^3] mobile apps has been released with several fixes and updates:

- Update alert/link on update, for those using the APK download application.
- Fix: display trade info with deleted user.
- Fix: buy ad wrong warning.
- Fix: chat buttons - increased pressable area.
- Fix: if the trade counterparty deleted the trade page doesn't load.

The full list of changes and the APK downloads are available on Github[^1].

Consult the previous Monero Observer report[^4] to learn more about the apps.
