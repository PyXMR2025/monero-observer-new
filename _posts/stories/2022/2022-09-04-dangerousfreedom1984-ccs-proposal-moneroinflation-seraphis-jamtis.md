DangerousFreedom1984[^1] has submitted a new CCS proposal[^2] to finish building the *Monero Inflation Checker*[^3] website and help with the development of Seraphis[^4]/Jamtis[^5]:

> I would like your help to finish developing www.moneroinflation.com by providing parallel implementations in Rust of the core functions in C++, improving the website and help with the development of Seraphis/Jamtis.

```
Total funding: 112 XMR (25h/week).

ETA: 18 weeks.
```

Consult the dev's previous CCS proposal[^6] to learn more about their work.

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !341[^2].
