The new Piconeroj.eu[^1] XMR/WOW P2P marketplace is now ready for beta testers[^2]:

> The site, in principle, is a glorius image board with Advertisements instead of Threads with a spirit of Craig's list [..] We are currently in alpha state and is in need to people to test out their buy/sell ads. 

The site is based on the open source TinyIB[^3] imageboard software. Although a hidden service is currently not available, there are no cookies or JS and users can access and use it via Tor.

Read the *Global Rules*[^2] before interacting with the platform.
