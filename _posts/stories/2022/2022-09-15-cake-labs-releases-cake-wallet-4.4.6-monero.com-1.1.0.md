Cake Labs[^1] has released *Cake Wallet* v4.4.6 and *Monero.com* v1.1.0[^2]:

> Huge exchange update! Auto rate selector for best rates and deepest liquidity, 15 additional assets, additional bugfixes[^3]

### Changes

    Automatic rate selector.
    Add 15 assets to exchange: APE, AVAX, BTT, DOGE, FIRO [..]
    Enable exchange for Haven wallets (Cake Wallet only)
    Simple Swap
    Trade details UI update
    Cake Pay fixes [..]

Consult the Github repository[^2] to read the full list of changes, verify and download the wallet APKs.

Note that Monero.com[^4] (iOS[^5], Android[^6]) is a Monero-only wallet and Cake Wallet[^7] (iOS[^8], Android[^9]) is a multi-coin wallet.
