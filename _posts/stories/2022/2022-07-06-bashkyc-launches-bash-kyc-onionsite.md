bashkyc[^1] has launched *The Boating Accident Support Hotline*[^2], an onionsite for KYC-free software and services: 

> BASH-KYC is a collection of non-KYC wallets, exchanges, and privacy tools for cryptocurrency, namely Bitcoin and Monero.[^3]

A clearnet mirror[^4] and the site's source code[^5] are available on Github.

To support the project, you can donate to the XMR address[^2] listed at the bottom of the page.
