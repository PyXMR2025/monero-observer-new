Cake Labs[^1] has released *Cake Wallet* v4.5.3 and *Monero.com* v1.2.2[^2] with new *advanced privacy settings* and several bug fixes:

> This update adds new "Advanced Privacy Settings" on wallet startup, which allows you to disable the fiat API, disable the exchange, and add your own custom remote node so you never need to ping our servers at all![^3]

## Changes overview

- New Settings pages (Connection, Privacy, Display, Other)
- PIN timeout setting (default 10 minutes)
- Allow Disabling Exchange in privacy settings
- Advanced Privacy Screen on wallet creation
- Allow disabling of fiat 
- Fix Duplicate Nodes
- ChangeNOW improvements
- Fix restoring backup with 6 PIN-code
- [..]

Consult the Github repository[^2] to verify and download the wallet APKs.

Note that Monero.com[^4] (iOS[^5], Android[^6]) is a Monero-only wallet and Cake Wallet[^7] (iOS[^8], Android[^9]) is a multi-coin wallet.
