The next *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled to take place on Saturday, November 12th 2022 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^2] Matrix & IRC channels and the agenda is available on Github[^3].

### Agenda

- Greetings
- Review budget for Prague, Czech Republic[^4]
- Review budget for Neuchâtel, Switzerland[^5]
- Vote
- Deadlines
- AOB
- Confirm next meeting time and date

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^6].

**Update 22/11/5: issue and agenda are now available.**

**Update 22/11/7: agenda updated (added budget).**

**Update 22/11/12: meeting logs available[^7]; everyone voted for the same venue option: *C) Prague, but fall back to Neuchâtel if funding target not met by a set deadline. If target does not cover Neuchâtel expenses, forward all funds to 2024*.**
