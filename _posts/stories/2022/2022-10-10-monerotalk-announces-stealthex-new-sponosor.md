Monero Talk[^1] has announced[^2] StealthEX[^3], a non-custodial *instant* cryptocurrency exchange, as their new sponsor.

To learn more about StealthEX, watch the latest Monero Talk episode[^4] which features an interview with the project's CMO Maria Carola:

> [..] being a sponsor for Monero Talk, which is fantastic, and I'm super stoked about it and I hope that this is gonna be the partnership that will last for many more months, years (Maria @ 5:50)

StealthEX joins Cake Wallet, Monero.com Wallet, and IVPN as Monero Talk sponsors.
