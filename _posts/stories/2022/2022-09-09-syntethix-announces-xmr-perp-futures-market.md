Synthetix[^1] has announced[^2] the addition of a new XMR perpetual futures market (sXMR)[^3] on the Kwenta[^4] protocol:

> XMR Perpetual market will join the growing list of supported cryptocurrencies available for leverage trading through Synthetix Perpetual Futures.

XMR can be traded with up to 10x leverage on the Synthetix-powered trading frontend. 

Consult the Kwenta docs[^5] to learn more about the decentralized derivatives trading platform.
