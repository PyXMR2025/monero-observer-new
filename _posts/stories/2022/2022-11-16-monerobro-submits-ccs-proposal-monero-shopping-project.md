MoneroBro[^1] has submitted a CCS proposal[^2] looking to develop and market the *Monero.Shopping*[^3]'[^4] project:

> A service that brings Monero one step closer to widespread adoption by enabling everyone to use Monero anywhere on the internet. Monero.Shopping allows everyone to spend Monero on any marketplace or e-commerce store, while providing an extra layer of privacy!

```
Total funding: 975 XMR.

ETA: 6 months.
```

### Milestones

- M1: Development - Invoice Automation (81.25 XMR)
- M2: Development - Fulfillment Automation/Optimization 1 (81.25 XMR)
- M3: Development - Fulfillment Automation/Optimization 2 (81.25 XMR)
- M4: Development - Implement Escrow 1 (81.25 XMR)
- M5: Development - Implement Escrow 2 (81.25 XMR)
- M6: Development - Implement returns 1 (81.25 XMR)
- M7: Development - Implement returns 2 (81.25 XMR)
- M8: Development - Browser Plugin Chrome (81.25 XMR)
- M9: Development - Browser Plugin Firefox (81.25 XMR)
- M10: Development - Allow Monero purchase via fiat 1 (81.25 XMR)
- M11: Development - Allow Monero purchase via fiat 2 (81.25 XMR)
- M12: Marketing - Social Media Ads/Influencer outreach and further optimizations (81.25 XMR)

According to the website[^3], the service fee is *50$+5%*, the order process may take up to 24 hours, and there will be a *no return* policy.

To read the full proposal, share your feedback, ask questions and support this CCS, consult !360[^2].

*This is an ongoing story and the report will be updated when new information is available.*
