*Exodus Wallet*[^1] has released version 22.8.26 for desktop[^2] with support for the recent Monero network upgrade[^3]:

> XMR users on desktop can now send, receive, and exchange XMR once again.[^4]

Note that the upgrade for *Exodus Mobile*[^5] and Trezor[^6] is *still being worked on*.

The delay was previously announced by an Exodus staff member, as reported[^7].

*Note that Exodus Wallet is not open source[^8].*
