In this Monero Talk episode[^1]'[^2], Douglas Tuman[^3] interviews *NYS Assemblyman*[^4] Clyde Vanel[^5], on the legislative environment around Monero in New York State:

> [..] is Monero really digital cash? [..] you can't follow that cash like you can with electronic transactions. If that's the case, if we wanna retain that in the electronic world then there's a place for Monero. (Clyde @ 15:30)

Several other topics are also discussed in this interview, including the lobby against crypto, capital gains tax, the utility of cash and the future of PoW mining in NY.
