The next *MoneroKon 2023 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled[^2] to take place on Saturday, January 14th 2023 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^3] Matrix & IRC channels and the agenda is available on Github.

### Agenda overview

- Greetings
- Update on CCS[^4]
- MoneroKon Sponsorship Policy[^5]
- Confirm Conference Sponsorship Packages offerings[^6]
- Community approval of invoices[^7]
- AOB [..]

Logs for the previous meeting are available on Monero Observer[^8].
