Haveno[^1] has announced[^2] Beldex[^3] project as their new sponsor:

> We welcome our newest sponsor: @BeldexCoin! Thanks for supporting #Haveno and our efforts for a decentralized, peer-to-peer and bankless finance!

According to their about page, Beldex *aims to restore privacy on the internet by building an ecosystem of decentralized privacy-first applications*[^4].

Beldex joins Cake Wallet, MajesticBank, Samourai Wallet and others as Haveno sponsors. To view the complete list, visit Github[^5].
